About

Everything I do
comes from the
same place.

I write. I build. I lift. I photograph. I study philosophy and close real estate deals. I've walked runways and sat in silence for hours. Not because I can't choose — because it all comes from the same thread: the pursuit of inner purpose, and the discipline to act on it.

None of it is separate. The body informs the spirit. The art informs the business. The philosophy informs everything. I'm not many things — I'm one thing, expressed in many directions.

This site is the center of that. Everything connects here.
